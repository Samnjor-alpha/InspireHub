Nauli is a fleet revenue management and expense management software service aiming to streamline collection of fares, parcel and luggage fees in the public service vehicle sector within the East Africa market.

Nauli Features

Revenue Collection Reports

This reports provides insights on the various sources of revenue for the organization, such as Fare,Parcels & Luggage.

Mobile Application

A Mobile App is provided for the Conductor,Luggage Manager & Parcel Manager.

SMS Notifications

SMS for E-Receipting, Parcel notifications, and document expiry reminders.

Route Perfomance

Identifies areas of improvement and optimize transportation routes for maximum efficiency and cost-effectiveness.
